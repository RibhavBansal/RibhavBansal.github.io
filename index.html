Flagship project

KahanDekhu

PWA · Android · Cloudflare Workers

An India-first "where to watch" tracker — type a movie or show, see every legal streaming platform it's on (subscription, free, rent, or buy). Live in production on Cloudflare's free tier, with push notifications, a WhatsApp bot, and a Play Store Android app.

01

Where-to-watch aggregation

A TMDB proxy Worker hides the API key and edge-caches search/trending/detail responses, surfacing JustWatch availability grouped into Subscription / Free / Rent / Buy across 8 supported regions.

02

Multi-channel reach

Web Push (VAPID + a Workers cron that re-checks availability every 6h) and a reply-only WhatsApp bot let people get "where to watch" answers without ever opening the app.

03

Accounts & sync

Optional Supabase email/password auth with Row-Level Security; watchlist, region, and service preferences merge across devices on sign-in, with a self-serve account-deletion flow.

04

PWA → Android

Installable PWA with an offline-first service worker, manifest, and icons — packaged as a TWA with domain-verified deep links for Play Store distribution.

MeetingMind

iOS · Chrome · FastAPI

An AI meeting assistant spanning iOS, Chrome, and a FastAPI backend — live transcription in 20+ languages, grounded Q&A across all your meetings, and real-time insight engines.

01

Multi-client capture

iOS live mic transcription plus a Chrome extension (Manifest V3, tabCapture/offscreen) for Google Meet — both feeding a shared FastAPI + SQLite backend.

02

Grounded retrieval

Full RAG pipeline (chunking → embeddings → vector store → cosine retrieval) with on-device TF-IDF fallback; GraphRAG over a 9-entity / 9-relation knowledge graph using BFS, community detection, and PageRank for cross-meeting Q&A.

03

Insight engines

Meeting DNA, commitment-drift, contradiction detection, and AI critique — each with an on-device fallback, surfaced in a live dashboard and in-meeting overlay.

04

Resilient orchestration

Four LLM/ASR backends (Gemini, Claude, Whisper, Mistral-7B via Apple MLX) with per-call failover, map-reduce summarization, and a rule-based $0 Demo Mode.
